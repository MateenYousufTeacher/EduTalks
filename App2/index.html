About This App

🏛️ Concept

Step into the role of a civilization leader across history. Every turn, AI generates a unique scenario — wars, trade, diplomacy, disasters. Your decisions shape your empire's fate and Wisdom Score.

🤖 AI Engine

Powered by Google Gemini AI. Every scenario, consequence, and feedback is uniquely generated — no two playthroughs are ever the same. AI builds context across your session.

How to Play
1

Read the Scenario

AI generates a unique historical civilization challenge. Read the context carefully.

2

Make Your Decision

Choose from 4 AI-generated options. Each path has different consequences for your civilization.

3

AI Evaluates & Responds

Gemini AI analyzes your choice and generates consequences, historical parallels, and wisdom insights.

4

Build Your Wisdom Score

Earn up to +100 points per turn. Good decisions compound. Reach 1000 for Legendary status.
