📱 About This App

Synonym Snap is a free offline educational Progressive Web App (PWA) designed to help students master English synonyms through interactive quizzes with instant feedback.

🎯 Features

  • 250+ synonym questions across 3 levels
  • Instant right/wrong feedback
  • Score tracking & streaks
  • Leaderboard with top scores
  • Bookmark difficult words
  • Works 100% offline
  • Installable on Android & Desktop

📚 Levels

  • 🟢 Easy – 100 basic vocabulary words
  • 🟡 Medium – 80 intermediate words
  • 🔴 Hard – 70 advanced words
  • 🔥 Bonus – 30 mixed practice questions

🛠️ Technology

Built with pure HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. No external dependencies. Full PWA with offline caching via Service Worker.
