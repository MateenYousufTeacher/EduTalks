🎮 How to Play

Each round, you run your startup by making 3 key decisions:

💲 PriceSet your selling price (₹10–₹100). Higher price = more revenue per unit but less demand. 📦 QuantityDecide how many units to produce (0–100). Each unit costs ₹5 to make. 📢 MarketingSpend on advertising (₹0–₹200) to boost customer demand.
📐 How Scoring Works
🔢 Final Score = Total Profit + (Avg Satisfaction × 50)
